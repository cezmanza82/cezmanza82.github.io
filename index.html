About me

Not just a web developer.

I'm Cezar, a BS Computer Science graduate with over 10 years in IT infrastructure — webhosting technical support, Linux administration and training, and server operations. Today I work as an IT Operations Team Lead.

Here's what that decade taught me: I've spent years fixing websites other people built badly. Stores on cheap shared hosting that crash on a busy day. Sites with no backups, no security, no SSL — built by developers who disappeared after getting paid. I've seen every way a website can fail, because cleaning up those failures was my job.

So when I build a store, I build it the way an engineer would — properly hosted, secured, backed up, and maintainable for years. I even built my own e-commerce platform, Winshap, from the ground up, because Filipino sellers deserve something better than renting shelf space from a marketplace.

I'm not an agency. When you message me, you talk to the person who will actually build and maintain your site — and who won't vanish after launch, because keeping systems running is what I've done my whole career.
