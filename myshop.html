What's included

Everything a real store needs

No plugins to buy. No extensions to configure. It all ships in one install.

Product Management

Add products with variants (size, color, caliber), pricing tiers, SKUs, stock tracking, and bulk import. Auto cost + markup pricing built in.

Order Management

Full order lifecycle from checkout to delivered. Status updates, tracking numbers, payment proof upload, and printable receipts.

Philippine Payments

Accept GCash, Maya, GrabPay, bank transfer, and COD. PayMongo integration for card payments. Manual payment proof upload.

Delivery Options

Configure multiple couriers with base fees, per-kg rates, and coverage areas. J&T, LBC, Grab Express — add any provider you use.

Customer Accounts

Registration with email verification, order history, wishlist, address book. ID verification system included for regulated products.

Admin Dashboard

Revenue overview, recent orders, inventory alerts, customer list, coupon management, banners, and full audit log with geo-IP tracking.

Age Gate PRO

Built-in age verification gate for airsoft, vaping, alcohol, or any age-restricted shop. Fully configurable, remember-me support.

3 Marketplace Templates PRO

FlashMart (Shopee-style), BazaarPro (Lazada-style), DealZone (Amazon-style). Customers feel right at home.

Smart Notifications

Email alerts for new orders, low stock, failed logins, brute-force attacks, and new login locations. All configurable per event.

Security built in

Not an afterthought

Enterprise-grade protection included on every plan.

Geo-IP Audit Log

Every login shows city + country. Know immediately if a foreign IP accesses your store.

Brute-Force Lockout

5 failed login attempts = 15-minute IP block. Email alert fires to admin immediately.

New Location Alert

Login from an unrecognized IP? Instant email alert to admin. Flag before damage is done.

2FA / OTP Login

Optional two-factor authentication. OTP sent by email before access is granted.
