What's included

Everything a real store needs

No plugins to buy. No extensions to configure. Everything ships in one install.

Product Management

Add products with variants (size, color, flavor), pricing tiers, SKUs, stock tracking, and bulk import. Auto cost + markup pricing built in.

Order Management

Full order lifecycle from checkout to delivered. Status updates, tracking numbers, payment proof upload, and printable receipts.

Philippine Payments

Accept GCash, Maya, GrabPay, bank transfer, and COD. PayMongo integration for card payments. Manual payment proof upload. PayMongo charges 2.2% + ₱10 per card transaction — this is their fee, not ours.

Delivery Options

Configure multiple couriers with base fees, per-kg rates, and coverage areas. J&T, LBC, Grab Express — add any provider you use.

Customer Accounts

Registration with email verification, order history, wishlist, address book. ID verification system included for regulated products.

Admin Dashboard

Revenue overview, recent orders, inventory alerts, customer list, coupon management, banners, and full audit log with geo-IP tracking.

Age Gate PRO

Built-in age verification gate for airsoft, vaping, alcohol, or any age-restricted shop. Fully configurable with remember-me support.

3 Marketplace Templates PRO

FlashMart (Shopee-style), BazaarPro (Lazada-style), DealZone (Amazon-style). Customers feel right at home.

Smart Notifications

Email alerts for new orders, low stock, failed logins, brute-force attacks, and new login locations. All configurable per event.

Security built in

Not an afterthought.

Bank-login-style protection included on every plan — not sold as an add-on.

Geo-IP Audit Log

Every login shows city + country. Know immediately if a foreign IP accesses your store.

Brute-Force Lockout

5 failed login attempts = 15-minute IP block. Admin email alert fires immediately.

New Location Alert

Login from an unrecognized IP? Instant email to admin. Flag the threat before damage is done.

2FA / OTP Login

Optional two-factor authentication. OTP sent by email before access is granted.

The fine print — in plain language
  • What's locked: the license renewal fee for the plan you signed up with, for one store. Setup fees, plan upgrades, and custom development are quoted at the rates current at that time.
  • Staying locked: the price lock holds as long as your license stays active. If it lapses for more than 6 months, rejoining is at the then-current pricing.
  • If Winshap ever winds down: your store keeps running — it lives on your own server, not ours. Lifetime licenses stay valid forever and you keep the final version of the software. No platform shutdown can take your store offline.
  • Direct developer access means same-day replies on business days — honest help from the person who built it, not a 24/7 hotline.
