Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Overview

czerwin ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website czerwin.net and uses automated tools to publish content on our Facebook Page. This privacy policy explains how we handle data in connection with these activities.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data from visitors to czerwin.net beyond standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited). These logs are used solely for security and performance monitoring and are not shared with third parties.

3. Facebook Integration

We use the Facebook Graph API to publish posts to our Facebook Page (Czerwin.net - From Idea to Online). This integration is used exclusively for automated content publishing. We do not collect, store, or process any data from Facebook users through this integration.

The Facebook app (czerwin-n8n) accesses only our own Page and does not access any user data, messages, or third-party information.

4. Cookies

czerwin.net does not use cookies or tracking technologies. We do not run analytics, advertising pixels, or third-party tracking scripts.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for information on how GitHub handles data.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data. Server logs are automatically deleted after 30 days.

7. Your Rights

If you have any questions or concerns about how we handle data, you may contact us at cezar@czerwin.net.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
